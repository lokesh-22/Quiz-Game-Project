Welcome to PokeQuiz

  • There will be 10 questions where you will be asked to guess the pokemon name from the image.
  • There are 4 options and only one option is correct.
  • Each question will have a timer of 10 seconds.
  • Each correct answer will give +4 points.
  • Each wrong answer will deduct -1 points.
  • If times runs out and question is unanswered, it will result in +0 points.
  • You'll get 3 lives and each incorrect answer will deduct 1 life
  • Scores will be displayed after the end of 10 questions.
